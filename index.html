The Challenge

Academic planning should not feel like guesswork.

Students must balance prerequisites, passed and failed courses, remaining credit hours, degree requirements, and course sequence—often using scattered or informal advice.

⛓

Complex prerequisite chains

One delayed prerequisite can affect several future semesters.

🗂

Scattered information

Academic details and university links are spread across multiple places.

?

Uncertain course choices

A technically valid selection may still create an unbalanced semester.

↗

Hard-to-find platforms

New students may struggle to find E-Learning, Teams, and Edugate.

The Solution

One clear path for academic progress.

Masar brings academic-plan analysis, semester recommendations, chatbot guidance, and university links into one responsive platform.

✓
Understand progress

Convert uploaded academic information into a clear summary.

✓
Plan the next semester

Suggest suitable course combinations based on prerequisites and remaining requirements.

✓
Explain recommendations

Use an AI chatbot to clarify why courses were suggested.

Unified Student Experience

From academic record to actionable roadmap

Masar follows a simple journey: upload, analyze, recommend, and explain—while keeping the interface clear and student-centered.
